This repository hosts the prototype design and foundational assets for the **Volkswagen AutoSync Companion App**. This application is designed to revolutionize the driver experience by integrating a powerful, conversational **AutoSync AI Assistant** directly into the vehicle management interface.

The core objective is to create a seamless, proactive, and intelligent connection between the driver, their vehicle diagnostics, and Volkswagen's service network.

## ðŸŒŸ Core Features

The AutoSync App unifies four major service pillars into a single mobile interface:

### 1. Conversational AI Service Hub (AutoSync)

* **Proactive Diagnostics:** Users can ask natural language questions (e.g., "How is my engine health?") and receive immediate, plain-language reports based on real-time vehicle data.
* **Intelligent Alerting:** The system automatically flags and explains critical alerts, such as low fluid levels or worn brake components, and suggests the next immediate action.
* **Frictionless Booking:** The AI assistant guides the user from a diagnostic alert directly into the service appointment scheduling flow, recommending convenient slots and authorized centers.

### 2. Digital Service Management

* **Real-Time Parts Inventory:** Users can query the availability and local pricing of specific parts (e.g., brake pads, engine oil) at nearby Volkswagen service centers.
* **Instant Payment Access:** Outstanding service balances are displayed directly in the chat interface, offering one-tap digital payment options (Credit/Debit Card, UPI).
* **Secure Document Wallet:** Centralized and secure storage for essential vehicle documentation, including the Driving Licence, Insurance, and Service Receipts.

### 3. Remote Vehicle Control

* **Remote Climate Conditioning:** Users can adjust the cabin temperature remotely to ensure the car is comfortable before they enter (e.g., pre-cooling or pre-heating).
* **Security Controls:** Remote locking/unlocking and vehicle locator functionality provide peace of mind and convenience.

### 4. Seamless Support Handoff

* **Integrated Call Center Access:** Provides a one-tap transition from the AI chat session to a live human support agent for complex issues.
* **Automated Communication:** Service confirmations, appointment details, and digital receipts are automatically delivered via a secure messaging platform (e.g., WhatsApp).
